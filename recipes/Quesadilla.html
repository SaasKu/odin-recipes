Quesadilla

Description

A Mexican classic that'll keep you coming for more!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • fajita seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 green bell peppers chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • mexican four cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the peppers, onions, and chicken separately
  2. Lightly grease a baking sheet and preheat a broiler
  3. Mix the chicken with fajita seasoning then place it on a baking sheet
  4. Place the chicken under the broiler and cook the chicken for around 5 minutes
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  6. Heat oil in a large saucepan over a medium heat and toss in and stir the bell peppers,
    onion, and broiled chicken for around 10 minutes.
  7. Place the contents of the broiler into half a side of the tortilla and
    place the mexican cheese on top of the vegetable and chicken pieces.
  8. Close the tortilla over the contents and place the quesadilla onto the baking sheet
  9. Bake the quesadillas in the preheated oven for around 10 minutes
  10. Cut the quesadillas into slices and serve
