Butter Noodles

Description:

A simple to make pasta with creamy flavor and noodles that you can't resist!

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces of fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil
  2. Stir in the fettuccine, bring it back to a boil, then cook it for 8-10 minutes
  3. Drain the water from the pot then mix in butter, parmesan, salt, and pepper until its all mixed up
  4. Pour into a bowl and eat
